Moment of inertia, i = $2/5mr^2$

Torque $M = -Tr$          (-Ve as rotating clockwise)

$I\ddot \theta = -Tr \rightarrow  1/2 mr^\cancel{2} \ddot\theta = -T\cancel{r}$

$T = -1/2 mr\ddot\theta$ 

By 'Pizza slice theorem', $\ddot x = \ddot \theta r$
